Getting Started

  • First scan: Place the letter on a flat, well‑lit surface. Use Keep This Scan → Add Another Page or Done Scanning This Letter.
  • Quality tips: Avoid shadows; align edges; enable flashlight if needed. The app auto‑enhances and crops.
  • Multi‑page letters: Scan pages separately, then save as a single PDF in your inbox.
  • Summaries & Chat: After saving, open the letter for a concise summary and optional chat for clarifications.

Billing & Credits

Letteris AI uses a credit system for scans and chats. You can view remaining credit and usage in the app’s Billing Dashboard.

  • Purchases: Credit packs are sold via Apple In‑App Purchase or Stripe Checkout (test mode during beta).
  • Receipts: Apple receipts are in your App Store history; Stripe receipts are emailed.
  • Refunds: For App Store purchases, request directly via Apple. For Stripe purchases, email us below with your receipt.

Privacy & Data

  • Local‑first: By default, letters and PDFs are stored privately on your device. Optional cloud sync (iCloud or other) is user‑controlled.
  • Minimal data: We only collect what’s needed to operate the service and billing, per our policies.
  • Documents: Review our Privacy Policy and Terms of Service.
  • AI disclosure: Some features are AI‑assisted and can make mistakes. Please review important outputs.

Troubleshooting

Notifications aren’t appearing

Check iOS Settings → Notifications → Letteris AI → Allow Notifications (Lock Screen, Banners). Make sure Focus modes aren’t silencing alerts.

Scans look skewed or dark

Place the page fully in frame on a flat surface, enable the flashlight, and retake using Retake. Ensure the camera lens is clean.

Billing / credits not updating

Refresh the Billing Dashboard and ensure you’re signed in. If still incorrect, email us with screenshots and your latest receipt.

App crashes or freezes

Reboot your device, ensure you’re on the latest app version, and try again. If it persists, send us the steps to reproduce and your device model.
